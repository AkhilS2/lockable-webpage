Lockable is a personal productivity app for iOS that helps you take control of your screen time by locking distracting apps behind your daily habits. Choose the apps that distract you, set the habits you want to build, and Lockable keeps those apps blocked until you have completed every one. It's time to earn your distractions.

 

 

 

 

How It Works

1

Block Distracting Apps

From the home screen, select the apps that distract you most. Add keys to set what it takes to unlock them.

2

Complete Your Habits

Keys are earned by completing habits or tasks you set for yourself. Finish them all to unlock your apps.

3

Track Your Progress

Your stats are recorded to motivate you to keep up with your habits. Watch your streaks grow over time.

4

Quick Focus Sessions

Need extra focus time? Block your apps in the timer section for a quick distraction-free session.
